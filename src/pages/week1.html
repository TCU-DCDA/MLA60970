Lab 1 Focus

Learning Objectives

By the end of this lab, you will be able to:

  • Analyze academic content for digital remediation opportunities
  • Apply CRAP principles (Contrast, Repetition, Alignment, Proximity) to scholarly communication
  • Understand web reading patterns and how they differ from print media
  • Develop content strategy for transforming academic work into accessible web content
  • Create user personas and understand diverse audience needs for academic content
  • Plan the structure and approach for your digital remediation project
