What You'll Learn & Build:

This course teaches you to transform your writing and ideas into compelling web content that reaches real audiences online. You'll learn essential design principles (Contrast, Repetition, Alignment, Proximity) while building practical skills in HTML5, CSS3, and responsive design. Through hands-on weekly projects, you'll create a professional website that showcases your work and makes it accessible to everyone. The course emphasizes inclusive design, real-world workflow, and effective communication using industry-standard tools. No prior coding experience needed. By the end, you'll have a live website that demonstrates your expertise and understand how thoughtful design creates meaningful connections with your audience.

What Makes This Course Different

  • Content transformation focus - turning your existing writing into engaging web experiences that connect with real people
  • Design fundamentals first - mastering contrast, repetition, alignment, and proximity as your creative toolkit
  • Build-as-you-learn approach - four connected projects that lead to a complete, professional website
  • Audience-centered design - creating content that's accessible and meaningful to diverse communities
  • Industry-standard workflow - GitHub, version control, and collaborative practices used by professional developers
